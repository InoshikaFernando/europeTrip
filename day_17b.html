🚙 Geneva walking tour

1
Basilique Notre-Dame de Genève
Pl. de Cornavin, 1201 Genève
10:15 Sunday Mass · neo-Gothic basilica · across from Gare Cornavin
⏱ ~1 hr Mass 🚶 Park nearby
2
Old Town & Cathédrale Saint-Pierre
Cour Saint-Pierre, 1204 Genève
Climb the tower for panoramic views · Place du Bourg-de-Four for lunch · cobbled medieval lanes
⏱ ~2 hrs 🚶 ~15 min walk from Notre-Dame
3
Jet d'Eau & Jardin Anglais
Quai Gustave-Ador, 1207 Genève
Iconic 140m water jet on Lake Geneva · Flower Clock (L'Horloge fleurie) in Jardin Anglais
⏱ ~45 min 🚶 10 min from Old Town
4
Mouettes ferry across the lake
Pâquis pier · CHF 2 (Tout Genève transit ticket)
Yellow water-taxi ferries cross Lake Geneva · short scenic ride · part of TPG transit network
⏱ ~10 min crossing 🚢 multiple lines M1–M4
5
Bains des Pâquis (swim if hot)
Quai du Mont-Blanc 30, 1201 Genève
Public lake swimming jetty · cheap entry · classic Genevois summer experience
⏱ optional 1 hr 🏊 bring swimwear
6
Reformation Wall · Parc des Bastions
Promenade des Bastions, 1204 Genève
100m monument to Calvin, Knox & the Protestant Reformation · giant chess in the park
⏱ ~30 min 🚶 5 min from Old Town

⚠ Practical tips
  • ⛪ Notre-Dame Basilica is across from Gare Cornavin · easy to find · arrive ~10:00 for seating
  • 🅿️ Park at Cornavin or Plainpalais (cheaper) · Geneva centre has restricted vehicle access
  • 🎫 Buy a Tout Genève / TPG day pass · covers buses, trams, AND Mouettes ferries
  • 🍫 If Maison Cailler skipped yesterday · stop at Broc (A12, en route home) · closes ~17:00
  • 🇫🇷 Geneva is right on French border · French Switzerland — French language, francs not euros
  • 👜 Pack swimwear + towel for Bains des Pâquis (optional)
