⚠ Practical tips
  • 🎟️ PRE-BOOK Schönbrunn — Imperial Tour or Grand Tour, online at schoenbrunn.at · skip-the-line essential in summer
  • 🅿️ PARKING — 8th district is a Kurzparkzone (max 2 hrs business hours, no overnight on-street). Best garages near hotel:
  • → Otto Wagnerplatz Tiefgarage (3 min walk · 4.0★ · 24h · ~€4/hr walk-up)
  • → Votiv-Garage (7 min walk · 4.4★ · 24h · near Rathaus)
  • → DOWNLOAD APPS for ~60% off: Bmove or BEST IN PARKING — pre-book and pay €15–20 instead of €38–45 walk-up
  • → Avoid Floriani Garage (closer but 2.0★ · narrow spots · steep ramps · 2.10m height limit)
  • → Ask reception (Nikola) about hotel partnership rates — many Vienna garages give ~30% off
  • 🚇 U2 from Rathaus to Stephansplatz · U4 from Karlsplatz to Schönbrunn · day pass ~€8
  • 🍰 Café Sacher: order 'Original Sacher-Torte mit Schlagobers' (with whipped cream)
  • 🍽️ Tewa Naschmarkt: closes ~22:00 weekdays, 18:00 Saturday, closed Sunday — adjust accordingly
  • St. Stephen's: free entry to nave; tower climb + catacombs require ticket (~€6)
  • Vienna City Hall: usually open until 21:00 in summer evenings (free wander of square)
  • Apartment has free tea/coffee + small kitchen — quick breakfast before Day 6 drive
