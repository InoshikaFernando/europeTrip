⚠ Practical tips
  • 🎟️ ABSOLUTE MUST: pre-book Vatican Museums at museivaticani.va · 09:00 first entry slot · adults €20 + €5 booking fee
  • 🎟️ Don't fall for skip-the-line scammers outside — they sell you tickets you could buy direct from the Vatican site for less
  • 🚶 17 min walk from hotel — major advantage of choosing DaBrieBra Vaticano · saves 1 hr vs central Rome
  • 👗 DRESS CODE strictly enforced: shoulders + knees covered for St. Peter's · no shorts, miniskirts, sleeveless · bring scarf as backup
  • 📵 NO PHOTOS in Sistine Chapel · NO TALKING · guards do enforce · expect 5+ min wait at entry to chapel
  • 🚪 SHORTCUT: at Sistine Chapel exit, take the door for groups (right side) directly into St. Peter's · saves you re-doing security at the basilica entry
  • ⛪ Daily Mass at St. Peter's Basilica: weekdays 17:00 (Italian) · attend if Mass for the day · Sunday papal blessing 12:00 from window
  • 🍕 Lunch tip: walk 5 min east to Borgo Pio for actual pizza al taglio · skip Via della Conciliazione (overpriced tourist menus)
  • 🍦 Tre Scalini in Piazza Navona: famous tartufo (truffle gelato) · €8 sit-down or €5 standing · worth the price
  • 🎫 Castel Sant'Angelo separate ticket €15 · pre-book if possible · less of an issue than Vatican
  • 💧 Bring 2L water per person · summer Rome is HOT · refill at Roman fountains (potable)
  • 🚇 If exhausted: skip Castel Sant'Angelo / Piazza Navona, go straight back · this is a long day already
  • 🦶 Realistic walking: ~10 km total · break the day with the lunch sit-down · don't try to cram more sights
  • Vatican Gardens tour: separate booking, +€33, 2 hrs · skip with kids · standard route gives you garden views from corridors
