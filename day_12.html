⚠ Practical tips
  • 🚨 Day 12 is ambitious — Florence + Pisa + Montecchio + Perugia + Rome + Trevi in one day is 8+ hrs driving. Drop one stop with kids.
  • 🅿️ AURELIO LOCATION IS THE STRATEGIC WIN — Auri apartment is west of Vatican, easier parking than central Rome (ZTL fines)
  • 🚶 18 min walk to Vatican Museums — major advantage for Day 14
  • 🏛️ Perugia parking: use Mercato Coperto or Partigiani escalators · NEVER drive into the medieval centre (ZTL active)
  • 🏰 Castello di Montecchio Vesponi: free entry · ~30 min visit · NOT the same as Castello di Montecchio in Verona
  • 🛣️ A1 toll Florence → Rome ~€20 + Perugia detour adds ~€5
  • 🗿 Trevi Fountain at night: floodlit, dramatically less crowded than midday · bring coins for the throw
  • Drive plan: NEVER enter central Rome ZTL · GRA ring road exits direct to Aurelio
  • Day 14 Vatican starts EARLY — pre-book museivaticani.va for 09:00 first entry
  • Hotel parking: residential street · free or low-cost · ask reception for the best spot
