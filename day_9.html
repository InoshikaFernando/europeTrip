⚠ Practical tips
  • 🚂 STATION-SIDE HOTEL = huge advantage · 5 min walk · skip car for Venice day · trains every 5-10 min · €1.45 each way
  • 🚗 PARKING in Mestre: Via Podgora area has paid street parking · ask hotel for the cheapest garage · island parking €25-30/day
  • 🎫 ZTL warning: Mestre is fine for cars · DO NOT drive onto Venice island (Piazzale Roma is end of the line, parking €30+/day)
  • 🚤 Vaporetto day pass €25 · only buy if doing Burano/Murano · single ticket €9.50 · walking is faster for centro
  • 💶 Venice expensive: water (€3-5) · coffee at table (€8 vs €1.50 standing) · sit-down meals near San Marco €40+/person · go 2 streets back for half-price
  • ⛪ Sat 18 Jul Vigil Mass: Basilica San Marco 17:00 · arrive 16:30 · this fulfils Sunday obligation so Sun 19 in Florence is free
  • 🌅 Best photos: Ponte dell'Accademia at sunset · Punta della Dogana golden hour · early morning San Marco (before 09:00)
  • 🚸 With kids: skip Doge's Palace queue · prioritise Campanile (lift, fast) · feed pigeons in Piazza · gondola is €90 for 30 min (4-6 people share OK)
  • 🍽️ Cicchetti spots: All'Arco (San Polo), Cantine del Vino già Schiavi (Dorsoduro) · stand-up bar food, kid-friendly
  • ⚠️ Pickpockets: very common in vaporetto crowds and San Marco · phones in front pockets · backpacks in front
  • 🌡️ July Venice = 32-35°C and humid · early start (07:00 train) beats heat AND crowds · break midday at hotel A/C
